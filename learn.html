LEARN TO SAIL IN MALLORCA

Whether you want to pick up sailing as a casual hobby, improve your skills, take part in competitions, or become a skipper, Mallorca has many excellent sailing schools that can offer you what you want.

“I wanted freedom, open air, adventure. I found it on the sea” – Alain Gerbault

SAILING SCHOOLS IN MALLORCA

Mallorca Sailing Academy

Palma
Professional RYA certifications
Taught in multiple languages
Career orientated programs

Perfect for individuals of any level seeking intensive experience backed by industry-recognised certifications..

Courses:

  • RYA Competent Crew
  • RYA Day Skipper Practical 5 day course
  • Coastal Skipper
  • RYA Day Skipper Online Theory and Exam
  • RYA Coastal Skipper / Yachtmaster Offshore Online Theory and Exam
  • RYA Yachtmaster Fast Track Online Theory and Exam

Escola de Vela Calanova

Calanova
Professional RYA certifications
Taught in multiple languages
Youth & Family Programs

Perfect for children and adults to learn how to sail at their own pace.

What they offer:

  • Accommodation
  • Events
  • Activities
  • International Camps
  • National Sailing School
  • Charter

Real Club Náutico de Palma

Palma
Professional RYA certifications
Taught in multiple languages
Youth & Family Programs

Perfect for all to learn how to sail or compete in the Bay of Palma!

What they offer:

  • Schools
  • Competitions
